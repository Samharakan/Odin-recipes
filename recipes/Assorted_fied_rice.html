Assorted Fried Rice

Description

Assorted fried rice is a combination of fried rice and different things like various vegetables and meat, chicken, sausage or fish, which gives it a different appeal and taste.This is quite different from the everyday fried rice we know.

    INGREDIENT

  • 2 cups long grain rice
  • Carrot
  • 2 Spring onions
  • Eggs
  • Green bell pepper
  • Green peas
  • Cucumber
  • 3 Sausage
  • Chicken breast
  • Vegetable oil
  • Soy sauce

STEPS

  1. Begin by cooking the rice, avoid adding much water, you want the rice to be medium hard.
  2. Chop carrots, spring onions, green bell pepper, cucumber and sausageonto a plate.
  3. Chop carrots, spring onions, green bell pepper, cucumber and sausageonto a plate.
  4. Spice the chicken with salt, pepper and a little curry powder.
  5. Break eggs into a bowl and stir.
  6. This step begins once the rice is ready
  7. Put frying pan on fire and pour a little oil into it.
  8. Put the spiced sliced chicken into the oil and fry till it soft and cooked, that should be about mins under low heat
  9. Add beaten eggs, the vegetables and sausage and fry for a while.
  10. Add some of the cooked rice and stir with the vegetables together.
  11. Add some soy sauce and stir till it mixes well.
  12. After achieving uniform look, your done.
  13. Enjoy ! /li>
